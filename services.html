What we support

Support built around the work that matters.

Our support is shaped by what our partners actually need to run and grow their operations. We focus on building structure around the work—so it stays consistent, scalable, and well-managed as you grow.

Rather than offering fixed packages, we work across key operational areas and adapt our support based on your workflows, tools, and goals. Each function is approached with the same level of ownership, attention to detail, and accountability.

Customer Support

Customer service, handled with care and structure.

We support customer experience across voice, chat, and email—handling everything from basic inquiries to more complex cases such as disputes, escalations, refunds, and verification processes.

Our teams are trained around your workflows and tools, with clear processes for ticket handling, escalation management, and quality assurance. Whether supporting traditional industries or more complex environments like fintech and web3, we focus on delivering consistent, reliable support that reflects your standards.

Content Moderation

Structured moderation at scale.

We help teams manage large volumes of content while maintaining quality, consistency, and compliance with platform standards.

From image, video and text moderation to policy enforcement and escalation handling, we build workflows that ensure decisions are accurate and repeatable. Our approach includes clear guidelines, QA layers, and continuous feedback loops to improve performance over time.

Explore Content Moderation
Website Development

Website development and ongoing support.

We support website development and management across platforms; Duda and WordPress—handling both build and ongoing updates.

Whether working on individual projects or managing multiple sites at scale, we bring structure to how work is delivered, tracked, and maintained. This includes content updates, QA checks, and coordination across teams to ensure consistency and quality.

Explore Website Development
Project Management

Project and workflow management.

We provide operational support to help teams stay organized, aligned, and accountable. From managing workflows and timelines to coordinating across teams, we ensure that work moves forward without unnecessary friction.

Our role can range from structured project management to ongoing operational oversight—depending on what your team needs to stay efficient and focused.

Common Questions

Services, answered.

Common questions about how our services work and how engagements come together.

What services does aCXend offer?

Four core disciplines: Customer Support, Content Moderation, Website Development, and Project Management—each run on documented playbooks and measured against outcomes you care about.

Can we engage just one service or several?

Either. Some partners bring us in for a single function, others for a combination. Every engagement is scoped to what your operation actually needs.

How does an engagement typically start?

We begin with a discovery conversation to understand your goals, then align on scope, staffing, and reporting before bringing your team or workflow live—usually within a couple of weeks.

Do you provide coverage outside standard business hours?

Yes. Our operations run 24/7, 365 days a year, so support, moderation, and project workflows keep moving on your customers' schedule.
