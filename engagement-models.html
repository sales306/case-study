Engagement Models

How We Engage

Dedicated, managed, or hybrid support — dialed to the ownership and control your team needs.

How we engage

Structured to fit how you operate.

Every team works differently, which is why we don't take a one-size-fits-all approach to engagement. We structure our support based on how your operations run today—and how you need them to evolve.

Whether you're building from the ground up or optimizing an existing setup, our role is to bring the right level of ownership, structure, and flexibility to support your team effectively.

Staff Augmentation

Dedicated support, fully integrated.

Our staff augmentation model is designed for teams that need additional capacity while maintaining direct control over day-to-day operations.

We provide dedicated team members who integrate into your workflows, tools, and processes—aligned with both your internal KPIs and performance expectations. While your team leads direction, we stay actively involved in performance management, quality monitoring, and ongoing coaching.

This ensures that even as part of your team, performance stays consistent, supported, and continuously improving.

Explore Staff Augmentation
Managed Services

We manage the work, not just the people.

Our managed services model is built for teams that want a partner to take full ownership of a function—from execution to optimization.

We handle hiring, onboarding, and team management, while also building the structure behind the work. This includes developing SOPs, designing training programs, implementing QA frameworks, and defining the right metrics based on your goals.

We also manage reporting through structured updates and reviews, giving you clear visibility into performance while we handle the day-to-day operations behind it.

Explore Managed Services
Hybrid Model

Combining control with structured support.

For teams that need both flexibility and ownership, our hybrid model combines elements of staff augmentation and managed services.

This setup works well when certain parts of the operation require direct oversight, while others benefit from a more structured, fully managed approach. We align responsibilities clearly—so there's no overlap, no confusion, and no gaps in accountability.

As your operations evolve, this model allows you to adjust how work is distributed without needing to restructure everything from scratch.

Explore the Hybrid Model
How we make it work

Built on clear processes and accountability.

Regardless of the engagement model, our approach remains consistent.

We establish clear workflows, define ownership, and implement quality assurance processes to ensure the work is delivered to a high standard. We also adapt to your tools and systems, so integration feels seamless from the start.

As performance data becomes available, we continuously refine processes and provide structured feedback—so improvements are ongoing, not reactive.

Common Questions

Engagement Models, answered.

How to choose the right level of ownership—and change it as you grow.

What engagement models do you offer?

Three: Staff Augmentation (dedicated members embedded in your workflows), Managed Services (we own the function end to end), and Hybrid Teams (a balanced mix of both).

How do we choose the right model?

It comes down to how much direct control versus fully managed ownership you want. We'll walk through the trade-offs during your first conversation and recommend a fit.

Can we start small and scale later?

Yes. You can begin with a single role or task and expand ownership as trust and volume grow.

Can we switch models as our needs change?

Yes. The structure is designed to evolve with you—shifting between augmentation, hybrid, and managed support without starting over.
